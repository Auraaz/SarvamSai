Who He Was

Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba

1926 – 2026 · A Centenary of Grace

Sathya Sai Baba was born on November 23, 1926, in Puttaparthi, a small village in Andhra Pradesh, India. In a life spanning 85 years in physical form, He touches the hearts of millions across the world — through unconditional love, selfless service, and the simple, timeless call to become fully human. He is Ananta — without beginning, without end.

He gives the world five Human Values to live by: Satya — Truth. Dharma — Righteousness. Shanti — Peace. Prema — Love. Ahimsa — Non-harm. Not as philosophy — as practice. Not as belief — as being. He is present in every act of truth, every gesture of love, every moment of peace.

November 23, 2026 marks one hundred years since His birth. SarvamSai is our humble tribute to that centenary — a devotional object crafted in His spirit, made without profit, in the hope that His ever-present grace reaches every hand that holds it.

The Four Swaroopas

His Robes.
His Values.

Satya, Dharma, Shanti, Prema — four of the five Human Values of Sai Baba, each embodied in the colour of a robe He wore. The fifth Human Value, Ahimsa, is not symbolic — it is lived in the making of every figurine.

01
Saffron Robe · First Value

Satya Swaroopa

Embodiment of Truth

Satya — the first of His Human Values. Baba's saffron robe carries the fire of absolute truth, of renunciation, of a life lived without pretence. If Satya finds you — this collectible is your daily reminder: truth is not spoken, it is lived.

02
Golden Robe · Second Value

Dharma Swaroopa

Embodiment of Righteousness

Dharma — the second of His Human Values. Baba's golden robe radiates right action — duty performed without desire, service offered without expectation. If Dharma finds you — this collectible honours the path of righteousness He illuminates for all.

03
White Robe · Third Value

Shanti Swaroopa

Embodiment of Peace

Shanti — the third of His Human Values. Baba's white robe is purity distilled — stillness made visible, peace made tangible. If Shanti finds you — this collectible carries the silence He offers the world, a world that needs it most.

04
Crimson Robe · Fourth Value

Prema Swaroopa

Embodiment of Love

Prema — the fourth Human Value, and the one Baba embodies above all others. His crimson robe is love made flesh — boundless, unconditional, without exception. If Prema finds you — the rarest collectible. The deepest truth. Prema Swaroopa.
